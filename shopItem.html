LACAILLE is a sculptural planter shaped around the idea of exoplanets planets that exist outside our solar system and orbit distant stars. LACAILLE reflects this sense of distance and mystery, capturing the imagined textures and mineral contrasts these remote worlds might hold.

LACAILLE

Design:
LACAILLE is available as a single piece and is handcrafted from a refined mix of concrete and volcanic rock, blending the smoothness of polished stone with the warmth of natural hues. Its marbled surface, veined with soft browns and deep mineral traces, evokes the shifting landscapes beyond our solar system. LACAILLE rests on a dark volcanic base that grounds its celestial presence.

Color:
Warm brown marbling on a pale surface
or
Ivory white with warm terracotta and earthen brown marbling
